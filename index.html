About

I am a PhD student (combined Master–PhD track) at the School of Applied Economics, Renmin University of China (RUC), specializing in Energy Economics (B.Sc. 2019; combined Master–PhD from 2023, currently in the doctoral phase). My research focuses on new energy industrial policy and electricity market design — with particular interests in negative electricity price formation, time-of-use pricing mechanisms, the interaction between medium-to-long-term contracts and spot markets, and the policy implications of China's renewable energy transition.

I have participated in two NSFC-funded projects, contributed to policy reports for the National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC) and State Grid Corporation, and presented at the International Conference on Climate and Energy Finance. Selected publications appear in Energy Policy, Energy Economics, and Economics and Human Biology.

本人就读于中国人民大学应用经济学院，能源经济学专业 （2019级本科，2023级硕博连读，目前为博士生）。 研究聚焦于新能源产业政策与电力市场设计， 重点方向包括：负电价成因与机制、分时电价设计、现货与中长期合同的交互影响， 以及可再生能源转型中的产业政策效果评估。

曾参与两项国家自然科学基金课题，为国家发展改革委及 国家电网撰写政策研究报告，并在气候与能源金融国际会议上汇报研究成果。 部分研究已在 Energy Policy、Energy Economics、 Economics and Human Biology 等期刊发表。
